Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

About This Service

This SMS-based organizational assistant is operated by a single account owner and select trusted organization leaders. It is used to coordinate family, church, and volunteer community groups — sending event reminders, RSVPs, scheduling notifications, and carpool logistics. The service is strictly non-commercial with no marketing or promotional messaging.

Data We Collect

We collect mobile phone numbers solely for the purpose of sending group coordination messages. We may also retain basic message history to maintain scheduling records within the group.

How Your Data Is Used

Your mobile number is used only to deliver SMS messages related to the group activities you have opted into (event reminders, RSVPs, scheduling, and carpool logistics). It is not used for any commercial or marketing purpose.

No Third-Party Sharing for Marketing

Your mobile phone number will not be shared with or sold to third parties for marketing purposes. We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or any unaffiliated third parties.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on organizational activity. You may receive more messages during active scheduling periods and fewer during quiet periods.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please check with your mobile carrier for details.

How to Opt Out

You can opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive one confirmation message and then no further messages will be sent to your number. To re-enroll, reply START.

For help, reply HELP to any message.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: admin@example.com
